# Lead Qualifier AutoPilot 🎯

A Python-powered engine designed to solve the "Lead Fatigue" problem for small business owners. This tool automatically vets incoming inquiries based on budget, timeline, and service fit.

## 💼 Business Impact
- **Time Saved:** Eliminates manual vetting of unqualified leads.
- **Speed to Lead:** Responds to high-value prospects within seconds.
- **Clean Data:** Pre-formats lead data for easy import into any CRM.

## ⚙️ How It Works
1. **Intake:** Processes raw inquiry text from contact forms.
2. **Scoring:** AI evaluates the lead against pre-set business parameters (e.g., Budget > $1k).
3. **Action:** High-scoring leads get a priority notification; low-scoring leads get a polite "not a fit" automated response.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack
- **Python** (Logic & Data Processing)
- **OpenAI API** (Decision Making)
- **JSON** (Structured Data Output)
